---------------------------Sonic Generations Mod--------------------------
----------------------------QSS Restoration v1.0--------------------------

This is a mod for Sonic Generations that recreates QSS (Quick Step Slide and Quick Stomp Slide) glitch from Sonic Unleashed.

------------------------------------------------------------
Mod Usage
------------------------------------------------------------
-This mod works ONLY with HedgeModManager, SonicGMI is NOT supported!
-To use Quick Step Slide, hold down B and quick step
-To use Quick Stomp Slide, stomp in the air and hold B until you hit the ground
